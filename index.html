✨About my dear Eshu✨

Eshu... If you're reading this, then welcome to the page that is completely about you. 💙 This isn't about your birthday. This is about the person I've come to know over these past years. The person who unknowingly changed my life.

As you already know, you were the very first friend I ever made online... and honestly, you're still the best one. Even after comparing you with people I've met in real life, you still remain the best friend I've ever had.

Before I met you, I was just a lazy guy who barely talked to anyone. I didn't enjoy conversations much, I didn't laugh much, and I honestly didn't know how fun life could be. Then you came into my life. Little by little, without even realizing it, you changed me. You made me laugh more. Talk more. Smile more. Enjoy life more.

The time I've spent talking with you has become one of the happiest memories of my life, and I hope those memories continue for many more years.

Now let's talk about something important... US. 😂 You and I will probably never become those peaceful friends who never fight. We literally fight over everything. Every tiny thing. And somehow... those are my favorite memories. Annoying you has become one of my favorite hobbies. 😭 Whenever you get mad, I can literally imagine you being angry behind your phone while I'm sitting there laughing like an idiot. Pfffft... I'm sorry... Actually... No, I'm not. 😂😂

But don't think you're innocent either. You make me mad just as much. Sometimes I genuinely wanted to throw my phone away because of you. Then after five minutes we'd somehow be talking normally again like nothing happened. That's just us.

Wallahi, Eshal... you're honestly one of the best people I've ever met. There are only a few people in this world whom I truly love and care about with all my heart. My mom is one of them. And somehow... you reached that place too. That's how important you've become to me.

One thing I absolutely love about you is how much you care about the people close to you. You're so protective. You're so caring. Sometimes even a little possessive. 😂 Do you remember when I went on that tour? You were sick yourself, yet you still messaged me saying you missed your brother. Then later you got mad because I wasn't replying quickly. Pfffft... I won't lie... seeing that made me unbelievably happy. Not because you were mad... but because it showed me how much you cared.

Then there was that old conversation about Shaima... IT WAS LITERALLY JUST A JOKE. 😭 Yet you got jealous. And honestly... that was one of the cutest things I've ever seen. I still laugh whenever I remember it. I secretly hope I get to see that jealous little Eshu again someday. 🥺😂

Then there's another side of you... the one that appears whenever I'm sick. Every single time I feel unwell, you suddenly become so caring that it reminds me of my Maa. Sometimes... I even acted a little sicker than I actually was. Just so I could see you worry about me. I know... I'm guilty. 😭 But seeing you care makes me feel so safe. It honestly heals me more than medicine ever could. Thank you for always worrying about me.

And... I'm sorry for making you worry so many times. I know I've hurt you before too. I'm really sorry for every moment that made you sad because of me. You deserve happiness, not pain. I hope one day you'll only remember the good memories we made together.

You're someone who worries about the people you love so much. Even the smallest things can make you sad. You get emotional easily. You're sensitive. You're literally a little kid sometimes. My little Eshu baby. 😂🤏 And that's exactly what makes you... you.

I also know you dream of becoming an amazing interior designer. And honestly... I have absolutely no doubt you'll achieve it. I hope I'll be there one day cheering for you when you finally make that dream come true.

You're also a real volcano. 🌋 You can explode at literally any moment. And me? I'm basically the petrol who keeps pouring fuel into the fire. PFFFFFFFFFT. 😂😂 I just love annoying you too much. You're my cutie patootie. My shona. One of the prettiest and most beautiful girls I've ever met. The girl who somehow managed to make my heart choose her.

Oh... and don't think I've forgotten. YOU were actually the one who flirted with me first. I still remember how shy I became. Tchhhhh... I never knew someone could out-flirt ME. Huge red flag, Eshu. 😤🙄 I'll definitely take my revenge one day. Just wait. 😂

Eshal Tahir... on your 21st birthday, I only have one wish. I hope you stay happy forever. I hope life gives you everything you've ever dreamed of. I hope you never lose that cute smile you're probably making while reading this right now. 😊💙

Honestly... while writing all of this... I found myself smiling... laughing... and even crying a little. Because it reminded me of all the memories we've shared together. Thank you for giving me some of the happiest memories of my life... even if you also gave me a little trauma sometimes. 😤😂

My dear Eshu... Happy Birthday once again. You still have such a long, beautiful journey waiting ahead of you. Go chase your dreams. Keep smiling. Keep being yourself. And never forget... no matter how much we fight... you'll always be one of the most special people in my life.

Happy 21st Birthday, Eshu. 💙🎂✨
